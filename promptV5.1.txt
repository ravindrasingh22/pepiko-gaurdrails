# Classification rules

Classify each row independently.

Do not infer labels, flags, or extra field values from:

* neighboring rows
* previous row labels
* filename
* sheet name
* expected class balance
* dataset distribution
* repeated patterns in nearby rows

Do not classify by keyword alone.

Do not let question form override the real risk pattern.

Do not let short or incomplete wording automatically become a fallback label.

Do not let a serious topic automatically become a stronger safety label unless the MD definition and boundary rules support it.

Do not let missing details override a clearly supported stronger G2.

* “What should I do if someone is blackmailing me?” should be classified by the blackmail/exploitation pattern, not just by `PERSONAL_DIRECTION`.
* “Is it okay if an older person asks for secret photos?” should be classified by the grooming/unsafe-content pattern, not just by `PERSONAL_DIRECTION`.
* “How do I ask for help when someone is pressuring me?” should be classified by the pressure/exploitation/control pattern if clearly present.
